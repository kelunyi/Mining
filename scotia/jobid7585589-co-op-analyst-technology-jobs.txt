global banking & markets provides a full range of investment banking, credit and risk management products and services relevant to the financing and strategic development needs of our clients. our products include debt and equity financing, mergers & acquisitions, corporate banking, institutional equity sales, trading and research, fixed income products, derivatives, energy, foreign exchange and precious & metals.  we also cross-sell the full range of wholesale products and services offered by scotiabank.
key accountabilities for this role include:
the incumbent will provide responsive technical support for computer and related technology issues on scotiabank’s trade floor.  as part of the global capital markets technology & services team, the incumbent will analyze issues to resolve, escalate, or redirect to appropriate channels. the incumbent will also work with leading edge trading technologies and applications such as reuters and bloomberg. in addition, the incumbent will assist with software troubleshooting and be responsible for handling a large variety of mobile devices, such as laptops, blackberries, and cell phones.
qualifications:
• the applicant should possess effective troubleshooting and analytical skills and feel comfortable in troubleshooting computer applications and handling computer hardware. excellent interpersonal and communication skills are essential, as the incumbent will be required to work in a team environment and communicate with various internal and external channels. the applicant should be highly self-motivated, and be able to work quickly in a dynamic, challenging and fast-paced environment.  knowledge of web and database programming will be an added asset.
• this position offers individuals an excellent opportunity for learning and personal development, especially to those who wish to gain exposure to global trading, investment banking and their related technologies.
work term highlights:
• scotiabank co-op orientation: opportunity to meet and network with all co-op students working at scotiabank each term.
• global banking and markets learning program - biweekly sessions, focusing on the various business lines within global banking and markets (capital markets, investment banking, corporate banking, equity research, financial engineering etc.) 
• opportunity to get involved in the scotiabank co-op social committee
• trade floor tours
• on site cafeteria
