scotiabank global banking & markets provides a full range of credit, risk management and investment banking products and services relevant to the financing and strategic development needs of our clients.  our products include investment banking, mergers & acquisitions, corporate banking, institutional equity sales, trading and research, prime brokerage, debt products, derivatives, foreign exchange and bullion & base metals.  we also cross-sell the full range of wholesale products and services offered by scotiabank.
key accountabilities for this role include:
the incumbent will work on scotiabank’s trade floor, providing web development and technical support of web applications, including on-demand development of reporting functionality and enhancement of web-based systems.  as part of the global capital markets technology team, the incumbent will analyze business processes, liaise with various groups within the bank, and develop web-based systems to meet business needs.  the incumbent will also get exposure to financial products, as well as leading edge trading technologies and applications such as bloomberg and thomson reuters.
qualifications:
the applicant should possess effective programming and analytical skills. experience with web development (html, css, javascript), scripting languages (asp, php, visual basic) and sql is preferred. familiarity and experience with relational databases such as microsoft access is an asset.  good interpersonal and communication skills are essential, as the incumbent will be required to work in a team environment and communicate with various channels. the applicant should be highly self-motivated, and be able to work quickly in a dynamic, challenging and fast-paced environment.
