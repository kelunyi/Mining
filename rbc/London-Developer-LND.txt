purpose


 


join the global equities technology team in london to perform general development on multiple concurrent projects of varying sizes


 


the equities business in london is rapidly growing and as a result ge technology london has a requirement for a dynamic, proactive and hands-on developer who will do whatever is necessary to ensure projects are delivered on time and to a high degree of quality


 


 


primary responsibilities


 




work on a variety of projects to directly benefit the equities trading desk in london


some projects will require the writing of code to be deployed in production, other projects will be data analysis oriented


some projects will be highly tactical in nature to address immediate business needs, whereas others will contribute to the platform’s target state


identify strategic internal platforms for tactical deliverables, liaise with other teams to migrate solutions to strategic platforms on a longer term basis


engage with both the technology team and the business directly to capture requirements for immediate implementation


anticipate potential constraints/issues and manage expectations


provide accurate estimates to project manager


liaise with vendor development teams as necessary


work closely with qa and production support teams through full development lifecycle of deliverables into production


where necessary, train the business on delivered functionality




 


 


           


authorities, impact, risk


 




incumbent works under guidance of regional head of ge technology, emea


day to day prioritisation also provided by other members of ge technology team


operates with limited supervision




 


 


key relationships


 




reports to regional head, ge technology emea


relationships with london equities desk


relationships with other it teams and it vendors


no direct reports     




 


working conditions


 




base location will be either thames court or riverbank house, london


occasional overseas travel may be required


trading floor environment




 


education &/or experience


 


required


 




education to degree level


high level of knowledge and understanding of equities trading business (cash and electronic desks, dma and algo trading)


understanding of fix protocol


experience of working with equities trading desk(s)




 


preferred


 




experience of fidessa trading platform highly desirable


experience of ullink trading platform
