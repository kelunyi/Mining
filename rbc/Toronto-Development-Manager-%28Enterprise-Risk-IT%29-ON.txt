purpose


lead and oversee a team of technical resources responsible for the design and implementation of applications used in enterprise risk.


all responsibilities carried out in accordance with code of conduct, rbc values and guiding principles ensuring adherence to privacy principles.


 


primary responsibilities




lead by example, setting a high standard of integrity, work ethic, and quality


set a development agenda that aggressively delivers value to our business sponsors


complete ownership of applications, including strategy, communication and financials


work with peers to establish and maintain an effective technology landscape for enterprise risk it


drive adherence to required processes and standards and ensure that all required documentation is accurate and up to date


responsible for management of relationships with relevant software vendors, consulting companies and other parts of rbc


ensure that employees understand rbc vision, as well as support and reinforce targeted behaviours that contribute to rbc goals


set direction and vision for the team with respect to the functions of our sponsors and within the overall department objectives


manage staff. provide focus and clarity in establishing individual goals, drive performance management, support career development and reward strong performance




key relationships




reports to “director, enterprise risk it”


business partners: enterprise risk


technology partners: other application development teams and infrastructure partners within technology & operations, rbc
