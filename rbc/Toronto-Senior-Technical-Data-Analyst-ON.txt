purpose


the party data management & quality team has been formed to look at the proactive data maintenace of the core capital markets party repository. the holder of this position will be specifically accountable for the proactive and effective manipulation of various data sources and will also be part of the team ensuring the integity of the customer data held in core party repositories, with a focus on design and execution of complex queries, reconciliations, and data transformations in support of gcds overall.


 


primary responsibilities




design, execution, and ongoing support of complex data queries, reconciliations, transformations, and analysis across the client/party data domain, encompassing all capital markets systems holding a representation of counterparty data.


participation of both strategic and tactical project workstreams to improve party data quality, meet business, regulatory, or tax requirements, and delivery of the target operating model for party data.


work with consumers of cdr data within capital markets who raise daily issues in respect of cdr data quality.


manage requests from multiple consumers across dmo, the broader capital markets division, and business management


gain a thorough understanding of overall implications of maintaining data in both upstream and downstream systems


use appropriate tools, exception management processes, and user training, to champion accurate set up of all new cdr records in line with cdr standards


management of incoming vendor feeds and internal sources of core party data highlighting exceptions and application of these to core party repositories


management of exceptions generated by regulatory requirements (internal & external) e.g., lrm, dodd frank, emir, fatca – exceptions resolved, production of appropriate metrics and root cause analysis


work with peers to minimise rework and redundancy across other projects


act as a subject matter expert on data management projects and work with data management project teams to understand implications of proposed changes to current processes


excellent team working capabilities and an ability to network to build strong business and team relationships.


must get involved and be able to shape and implement positive change


ensure escalation of any issues that can impact the budget or deadline




authorities, impact, risk




the   of the party data management & quality team is to improve efficiency and/or save money


counterparty reference data affects many areas of the bank and therefore the projects will cut across all areas


as reference data underpins most of what the bank does with an external client – risks to be managed can be material and significant




key relationships


 




reports to regional team manager


executives & senior managers of capital markets globally


all areas of the bank that use reference data globally


enterprise counterparts
