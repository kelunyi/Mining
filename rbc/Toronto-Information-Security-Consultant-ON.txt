Title:Information Security Consultant
Location:Toronto, ON, CA
Date Posted:2015-06-19
URL:https://jobs.rbc.com/job/Toronto-Information-Security-Consultant-ON/274759800/
purpose:


to provide information security and it risk expertise within rbc. to use this expertise in internal consulting and external representation with the objective of optimum protection of all rbc information assets.


 


 


primary responsibilities:


 


it risk and security consulting


provide consulting services to project teams and their business units to assist in their security design in order to identify  potential risks. review the security issues and recommend  the appropriate controls to mitigate these risks


participation in security and it risk reviews, walkthroughs, privacy assessments, and risk assessments


preparation of security risk assessments


participation in the security exemption approval process


provide security awareness to rbc groups when called upon


assess security of our rbc financial outsourcing initiatives including onsite inspections and review of physical and logical controls


provide support to other consultants through mentoring and peer reviews of work products as required


provide ad hoc it risk consulting services to the general user community


 


 


security standards and guidelines


participate in creation and maintenance of  rbcit risk standards and guidelines and ensure they reflect current best practices


participate in creation and maintenance of information security assessment tools and templates


 


 


review emerging security technologies & practices


awareness of new technology and techniques e.g. (electronic commerce, internet initiatives, cryptographic hardware/software, key management, security infrastructure)


ongoing professional development


 


position requirements/specifications


 


core :


business knowledge


information seeking 


analytical thinking


team work and co-operation


listening, understanding and responding


 


technical:


expert knowledge of rbcfg security policies and standards


expert knowledge of information security  practices


iso 17799


 


proficient knowledge of compliance, audit and privacy policies and regulations


general knowledge of security technology


access control products


authentication


network security


cryptography


general knowledge of rbc technology security standards & practices


 


cissp/crisc required – will be expected to complete  and maintain certification


 


how time will be allocated (percent):


security & it risk consulting                           80 %


security standards & guidelines       10


review emerging security technologies & practices   10


          


key organization relationships:


t&o executive, svp it risk, ciso,


directors, managers and project managers from t&o and other units.


 


reporting relationships: 


director security consulting, it risk
