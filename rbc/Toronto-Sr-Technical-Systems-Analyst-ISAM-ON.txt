responsibility for:


 




isam
: this is the next generation web access gateway, securing the browser and mobile access


legacy web access gateway, securing the browser and mobile access of rbc clients


datapower
: this esb integration platform runs multiple applications for all major rbc line of business


wmb (websphere message broker)
: this esb platform is used for integration and complex process orchestration, complementing datapower in building the rbc hybrid enterprise service bus




 


this position also provides technical consulting to application development groups on design and proper use of isam and rbaccess middleware


 


responsibilities:




provide development, production support, maintenance and technical consulting for ibm isam software components & infrastructure (primary)


provide development, production support, maintenance and technical consulting for rbaccess software components & infrastructure (secondary)


provide development, production support, maintenance and technical consulting for ibm datapower software components & infrastructure (secondary)


keep abreast of web access gateways solutions and standards in the market place to help identify key solutions that meet/exceed business objectives.


assist in defining application design, development and deployment guidelines & best practices for secure web access gateway infrastructure.


conduct design reviews when required.




 


position requires carrying a pager on weekly rotations for emergency support for isam and later on other technologies, also covering as a backup for project development and support when team members are on vacation.


 


 


how time will be allocated (percent):


40% evaluation, design, analysis and development


40% developers consulting, qa and production application support


20% research and technical planning


 


key organization relationships:


reports to manager, messaging middleware & web services infrastructure.


development community – developers and managers


technical & operational support teams


 


key competencies required:




expertise in isam product (3+ years).


familiarity with similar products desired but not required


familiarity with eai and esb technologies, such as ibm datapower, or similar (desired but not required)


experienced in internet and web technologies (min 3 years)


proficient c/c++/.net/java programmer. (min 5 years)


proficient xml (xml, xslt, xpath,.)(min 3 years)


experienced in web services technologies and ws-* stack  (ws-security, ws-trust, , ws-secureconversation,,saml) (min 3 year)


experience with enterprise formal development processes


good administrative understanding of unix, aix preferable.


exposure to information and systems security technologies


background in troubleshooting and providing support to a production environment.


proven self-starter who can identify possible work areas, assess the need and deliver a solution.


excellent communication skills (written, oral and presentation) capable of dealing with multiple development teams, including internal, off-shore or external partners.


strong service partner orientation


positive attitude toward technical issues and project pressure


information seeking, self sufficient in efficiently dealing with new technologies and technological challenges


innovation


team work & cooperation, networking inside the company for getting support in the day by day challenges


university or college degree in computer science
