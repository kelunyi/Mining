planning & strategy

be a single point of accountability for the success of the bsa, qa and release management processes

drive the implementation of the bsa and qa processes and assist with the overall process improvement within rbc insurance it

contribute to development and implementation of common bsa processes with continuous improvement


benchmark application group’s qa and bsa services to best in industry organizations


establish and drive strategic and tactical departmental initiatives and plans


partner with requirements coe, qa coe, pre-production services and other qa groups within rbc

researches emerging trends/best practices and determines implications of proposed methodologies on meeting business unit strategy


leads/directs the software automation effort by identifying applications that will benefit from automation and develop/implement test cases
