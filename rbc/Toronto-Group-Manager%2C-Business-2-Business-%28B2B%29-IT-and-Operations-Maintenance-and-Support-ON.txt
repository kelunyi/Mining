Location:Toronto, ON, CA
Date Posted:2015-06-17

-------------------------------------------
purpose


leads and manages the maintenance and operations of a set of diverse and highly complex systems and applications (5+) within payments & trade technology team with specific focus on b2b connectivity that facilitate the movement of file exchange with external clients.  it includes overall accountability for the safety and soundness of these applications, and ensuring new and improved enhancements are successfully integrated / implemented into the environment. 


 


this is a high profile leadership role that services multiple lines of business and operations (eo&p, bfs, retail etc) across the entire enterprise.


 


all responsibilities are carried out in accordance with code of conduct, rbc values and guiding principles ensuring adherence to information security and privacy principles.


 


****
overall accountability to set the vision and establish direction for the maintenance and support of technology solutions that are clearly aligned with the various businesses strategic objectives and priorities related to digital processing activities. 
****


 


managerial excellence / building high performing teams


 




leading advocate of rbc financial group values


promotes a mindset for sustained success, growth and diversity.


provide leadership, coaching, and direction to direct reports in creating a working environment of high employee engagement with focus on proactively providing reliable, flexible and innovative technology solutions.


attract, develop, retain and lead a team of highly skilled professionals


develops staff for current and future needs including back-ups for key maintenance and support positions ensuring a seamless transition to our business partners


expert in the application of the talent management process (recruitment/selection, performance management, performance development/coaching, reward/recognition), ensuring it managers are sufficiently equipped to support their respective teams


leads the strategy for planning / structuring the department to efficiently meet the rapidly changing expectations and needs of business and operations partners


spearheads and guides people through changes




 


client focused solutions / relationships & communication


 




ensures strong business and technology alignment while leading the maintenance and support of a portfolio of diverse and complex applications that are of critical strategic impact and organizational significance to the businesses.


communicate technology & operations key initiatives and processes to align with and support business strategy


provides superior client experience and establishes strong relationships with senior it and operations leaders.  collaborates with colleagues across the organization to get things done.


owns/manages the relationship with multiple business and operations stakeholders across the enterprise


drives / seeks out industry trends and organization knowledge to generate technology solutions that meet business expectations


directs all maintenance initiatives with an overall integrated approach at the business level.


key relationship manager and collaborator with a network of colleagues, service partners, clients and third parties.




 


 


governance & financial accountability


 




provides security stewardship to 5+ critical applications, ensuring the data pertaining to clients remains safe guarded


ensures that all maintenance implementations meet enterprise application and technology standards, and follow the prescribed enterprise governance framework and processes.


provides governance and oversight with respect to issues resolution, incident management, the incident communication framework


provides leadership and direction to continually improving efficiency in order to reduce maintenance/total cost of ownership associated to our technology.


manages the overall technology maintenance budget for the business ensuring the best possible return on investment for the client.


directly accountable for an annual planning budget, which includes; systems maintenance, vendors services and professional fees.


develops operating plans, establishes priorities and documents staffing requirements for current and future years.




 


 


improving efficiency and effectiveness


 




drives rbc’s vendor / partnering strategy to ensure optimal use of partners while maintaining a strong base of internal subject matter expertise


provides leadership and expert counsel in establishing and implementing standards, guidelines and best practices for the maintenance and support of b2b (and possibly a2a) technologies


establishes and manages in conjunction with architecture a technology currency roadmap


participates and contributes in the definition of overall systems architecture to support the business objectives and to ensure within connectivity technology that there is a consistent use of technology and architecture.


seeks out industry trends and organization knowledge to understand alternative approaches / solutions.


thinks creatively about opportunities to align rbc offerings with future client needs.


champions the creation of metrics and manages with a higher level of information i.e. at the trends and management indicator level; differentiates between project and program metrics.


overall governance & oversight of all maintenance and support vendor relationships


resolves ambiguity


implements best practices and continuous improvement programs aligned with rbc it strategies
