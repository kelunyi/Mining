Title:Associate Director - Risk Shared Services
Location:Jersey City, NJ, US
Date Posted:2015-06-24
URL:https://jobs.rbc.com/job/Jersey-City-Associate-Director-Risk-Shared-Services-NJ-07097/275669300/
purpose:  

this role manages the  it risk shared service function which includes acting as enterprise product owner of the bank’s it goverance, risk and complaince framework and application (archer), enterprise it risk reporting including providing trending and analytics, reporting and dashboards & program execution. 

 


primary responsibilities:




it governance, risk & compliance framework


operate the it risk framework


develop processes and procedures to operationalize the framework (i.e. internal processes and procedures)


develop, implement and monitor processes procedures to operational the framework with first line partners


product owner archer


product owner of the archer grc platform for the bank


manage the target operating model and roadmap for archer


chair both design board and centre of excellence committees


develop key relationships with development and support teams to build in line with the strategy and roadmap


enterprise analytics & reporting


manages the execution of it risk data collection and validation, data consolidation, retrieval, analysis and presentation processes across all first line areas.


supports the creation and communication of the it risk reports and metrics and contributes to the continuous improvement of the reports in line with industry best practices


liaisons with other it risk related groups to reduce overlapping it risk processes and reporting and to ensure consistent reporting results for operational risk


provide analysis of it risk related information to support identification of areas of concern or potential concern and recommend remediation. 


directs research into areas of perceived elevated or emerging operational risk, throughout all areas of t&o.


completes qualitative and quantitative analysis of internal and external operational risk information.


supports the implementation of tools to improve or automate reporting


maintain repository of it risk policies. 


program execution


run it risk governance initiatives and programs to deliver on the mandate


program all it risk governance initiatives including  grc framework & archer




 


 


position specifications:


required:




university degree, or equivalent


8 years in the financial services or other regulated industries


5 years’ experience in development of it risk frameworks


8 years of experience in the strategic development and support of archer


strong metrics and performance management background including data management and analysis


3-5 years in a delivery organization




preferred:




experience in working within a larger financial services company


knowledge of project management (pmf) process/disciplines
