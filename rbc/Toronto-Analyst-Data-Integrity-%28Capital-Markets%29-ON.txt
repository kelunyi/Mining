Title:Analyst - Data Integrity (Capital Markets)

purpose of the role is managing wider crm and other system data integrity issues


this role will offer a significant opportunity to act as a change agent leading efforts that will strengthen our front office client relationships. 


the crm strategy team will interact across global markets functions; the role offers the opportunity to move from operations to the front-office.




 


responsibilities:


 




liaison between crm strategy analyst and technology


support data integrity through data transformation and data clean up efforts including:  reviewing the reconciliation of various systems feeds (inputs/outputs) to the crm; conducting due diligence on data errors existing in crm and other systems (revenue) identifying discrepancies; following up and fixing errors


track key metrics on data integrity


grow data integrity efforts upstream from crm system to include other systems (e.g. rr codes in oms systems etc)


work with business stakeholders to understand data remediation priorities


ability to understand various trading flows to assist in the maintenance of data repositories


identify opportunities to enhance operations efficiency and data management and streamline existing processes


kick out revenue systems exceptions and manage resolution




 


data reconciliation and integrity management:  
ongoing maintenance/reconciliation between systems and management of client reference data


 




reconcile and maintain master accounts in front-office versus back-office versus database systems


implement credit hierarchy in cdr and add corresponding view in workbench


maintain client-to-plan mapping in csi


gather requirements for manual table administration and subsequent automation




 


data clean-up and maintenance:  


ongoing effort to clean up and maintain crm/systems data


 




eliminate duplicate and stale contacts


fix incorrect client naming conventions


separate issuer/client data structure


align naming conventions/structure with analytics reporting convention


delineate client versus prospect


manage media contacts


add pt ids


add coverage where missing


manage coverage contacts lists


add/manage mclaughlin codes


add/manage factset holdings




 


 


data issue resolution


 




investigate and trouble shoot data issues as they arise (e.g. templeton investments versus franklin global – revenue not showing up correctly)




 


qualifications


 




ba, ma, ms or equivalent with an in-depth understanding of institutional capital markets


3+ years experience working with crm system


demonstrated ability in written and oral communications


self startertexcellent verbal and written communication skills


ability to thrive in a fast-paced,  high stress and detail oriented environment


ability to facilitate meetings among stakeholders with diverse and sometimes conflicting points of view, including strong negotiation skills and developing strong client relationships
