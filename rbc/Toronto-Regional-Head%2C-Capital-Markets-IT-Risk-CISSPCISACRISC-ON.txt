purpose


 


a key member of the global capital markets it risk team with responsibility for management and delivery of our it risk practices for capital markets in canada.  key objectives include:


 




manage and operate risk identification and control processes across the toronto based capital markets it organization.


develop approaches to identify and monitor key controls aligned to the risk universe and regulatory requirements.


guide and influence the development of key risk indicators


maintain strong relationships within the technology team, business continuity, internal audit, operational risk and the it risk centre of governance functions within the bank.


manage programs to mitigate both operationally and project delivery risks and provide comprehensive management reporting.


drive the global team’s strategic direction.




 


 


primary responsibilities


 




provide technical subject matter expertise on all it risk matters which includes (but is not limited to) business continuity risk, information security, change management risk and project risk. 


drive the capital markets it risk practice forward by developing and implementing strategic, proactive technology risk management practices.


local reporting manager for a small team (1-2) of it risk staff


conduct top down risk and control assessments




manage the approach and framework and conduct interviews with senior managers within the organization and their directs to identify risk and assess the risk


provide recommendations and strategies to management to mitigate risk


ensure appropriate reporting is in place










conduct detailed (or bottom up) risk and control assessments




manage the approach and framework to identify specific areas of focus and conduct a bottom up technical risk assessment










conduct root cause analysis on incidents/events




based on needs, there will be a requirement to lead or participate root cause analysis of incidents to understand root cause and develop approaches to mitigate in the future. 










management reporting




produce  content for monthly reports/dashboards


develop  key risk indicators, thresholds associated with the risk universe










manage programs and contribute  to our strategic direction




on occasion, manage programs to reduce risk on behalf of the organization.


as a key member of the leadership team, contribute to the strategic direction of it risk










develop and maintain key relationships with various risk/control functional groups within the bank (i.e information security, audit, compliance, operational risk, it risk cg)




 


 


authorities, impact, risk


 




authorities identify and report on risk to the global head of it risk


authorities to recommend key risk indicators


authority to work with and make recommendations at a regional level


scope includes all capital market it functions based in the canada.
