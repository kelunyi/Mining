Title:Software Asset Manager
Location:Toronto, ON, CA

-------------------------------------------
purpose:


to support the direction and management of a defined set of supported software titles with the implementation of best practices from the itam software asset management program.  this professional will have a clear understanding of the current software licensing policies in place and manage expectations by meeting self-audit targets, on boarding new software titles and delivering quality customer service.  efficiently manage software requests by redeploying surplus where possible (to control rbcfg software expenditure), responsible for the day-to-day operational activities and will act as the primary contact for internal clients. be responsible to provide ongoing status reports as well as identify opportunities to streamline processes that will assist in the success of software asset management.


 


all responsibilities carried out in accordance with code of conduct, rbc values and guiding principles ensuring adherence to privacy principles.


 


primary responsibilities




manage software assets as they relate to the itam lifecycle for assigned portfolio of products/services and geographies, working towards consistent global coverage and responsibility. titles include (but not limited to): red hat (rhel, jboss), oracle (dbee, rac, tuning/diagnostics, partitioning, ocon, weblogic), tibco (businessworks), symantec (storage foundation), cisco (tidal, jaws), vitria (businessware), microsoft sever products (sql, biztalk, crm, windows server, exchange, sharepoint).


ensure process, procedure, issue tracking and relevant documentation is current and complete, in collaboration with the manager and the team


conduct/participate in regular meetings and collaboratively work with internal/external partners to on-board new applications and tools


prepares relevant documents related to purchases/true-ups and present to senior managers/executives for sign off


completes/enters ebudget records as required for financial tracking


liaise with senior managers and executives to socialize contracts and supporting documentation on software expenditures


establishes relationships with resellers and vendors to promote support and advice with software asset management


responsible for managing the activities related to the administration of software entitlements


assist in the acquisition of software solutions by interfacing with the vendors and the software support staff (procurement)


provide input on high dollar value contracts and negotiations


ensures that technical and management approvals are acquired for procurement, deployment and control of software assets


reconcile ongoing software requirements in a timely manner as it relates to the existing vendor agreements


completes software asset management reports including the monthly/quarterly/annual self-audits and other reports as required by governance and audit staff


act as a primary contact/referral point for both internal and external software audits


establishes and maintains accurate records of software including details of ownership and allocation


maintains detailed records of software deployments and relevant  agreements (in conjunction with the procurement group), which may be contained within an asset management database


ensure available software licenses are redeployed from surplus inventory prior to ordering new licenses from the supplier


review and discuss service level agreement targets quarterly with vendors


contribute to process improvement/re-engineering initiatives for administration, maintenance and management to ensure the reduction of total cost of ownership


participate with other team members in establishing team performance objectives and promote the concepts of team building


provide backup/relief for team members as required
