key accountabilities


 


this person will be part of a number of projects and responsible for the business analysis, documenting requirements, working with it and business to implement solutions, and assist in the definition and coordination of business testing.


 


primary responsibilities


 




lead workshops with stakeholder’s and sme’s to drive out business requirements


document business requirements


convert business requirements into functional specifications


data analysis including quantifying and defining cleansing activities 


operational process re-engineering


liaising with it to explain functional requirements and ensure technical solutions meet requirements  


define test scenarios for uat


define training strategy and assisting in preparation of training material 




 


education &/or experience


 


required




minimum 5 years working as a business analyst in a similar sized organisation within the finance sector


experience of reference data maintenance processes & controls in a large financial institution (clients/accounts, books, or securities)


a high level of business engagement and stakeholder management experience


track record of delivering similar data analysis work to a high standard




 


preferred




experience working on a global team


knowledge of the front-to-back office reference data workflows, systems, processes and controls


knowledge of reference data and mdm platforms


experience working on a know your customer (kyc) project


a working knowledge of key capital markets products and services


iseb business analysis or equivalent qualification
