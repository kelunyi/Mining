key accountabilities 


 


business analysis & consulting 


• given the global nature of the environment the individual may be required to work
 on a rotating 3 shift basis


• provides ongoing support services to business partners on applications (small to large of varying complexity).

• identifies, tracks and resolves business systems issues.

• recommends business solutions that satisfy sponsor/stakeholder needs continually exploring and assessing options for value-add for clients.

• ensures adequate end user documentation and training.

• contributes when required, to successful project completion within budget and on time by identifying risks and developing/recommending mitigation strategies.




planning & testing


participates in development of shift coverage, planning and provides recommendations for improvement in application support quality

• participates in the development of detailed plans and accurate estimates for completion of requirements and acceptance phases of projects

• participates in the development of test plans and executes testing including leading the systems and acceptance testing by facilitating participation of appropriate staff in project completion.




relationships & communication


• effectively communicates and builds rapport with team members, stakeholders and business partners using a variety of techniques and collaboration from initiation to close.


• may be required with reasonable notice to work alternate shifts for a specified duration or planned overtime as required by your manager.


• resolves conflicts/issues of medium complexity and escalates others as appropriate.




development & coaching

• may act as team leader.

• provides direction, expertise, feedback, coaching and development to build the capability of more junior staff.

• continually enhances skills and builds knowledge in all aspects of the organization, the business and information systems.




knowledge / experience


education/experience (to enter position)


• professional with experience on applications with multiple functions


• support experience in relational databases .net technology




process knowledge


prior application support knowledge utilizing peregrine , escalation processes etc

• requirements methods: interviewing, data modeling, business process modeling, business object modeling and user interface design

• quality controls

• cost/benefit analysis & alternatives identification

• communication skills

• facilitation skills

• presentation skills

• risk assessment & quantification methodologies

• conflicts and issues resolution and escalation




business knowledge

• capital markets business line(s)’ business applications/ processes.


• financial market experience, especially with fixed income and derivatives


• market data experience in term of pricing of securities


• gain and loss impact analysis


• bu specific strategies & critical success factors




systems knowledge

• end user reporting tools

• relevant technology

• it standards, methodologies, cmm & audit requirements




people management knowledge

• talent management process (recruitment/selection, performance management, performance development/coaching, reward/ recognition)


 


knowledge / experience 


• experience working with back office applications as well as process & procedure knowledge of back office operations and finance

• ability to work with both technical & business resources

• project management lifecycle knowledge

• previous experience working with ms office suite (especially access/excel)

• knowledge of capital markets products (i.e. fixed income, derivatives)

• knowledge/experience with accounting practices and procedures a must

• experience with databases and.net technology


 


education required 


ba/bsc
