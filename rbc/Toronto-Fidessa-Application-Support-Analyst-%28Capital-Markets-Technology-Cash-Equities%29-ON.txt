purpose:


 


the application support analyst will own the technical support for the fidessa application.  they will be supporting business unit/traders for cash equities application related issues.


 


 


primary responsibilities:


 




provide application support for institutional fidessa


scripting - using perl / shell / tcl  for system monitoring, and setting up of intra-day/eod reports


implement new releases, bug fixes/patches/configuration changes into production


liaison with trading and escalation to dev, network, systems, dba etc.


contact exchanges and brokers/vendors to resolve connectivity issues


investigate trading issues identified by operations and/or front-office control


problem solve issues through investigation of relevant application logs


monitoring of applications and pro-actively fixing any warnings


creating queries to run reports for compliance enquiries


provide early coverage, participate in weekend activity for street wide exchange and dr tests.




 


must have staffing requirements:




bachelors engineering/computer or related degree


3+ years experience in support


capital market experience and knowledge of equity markets


excellent oral/written communication skills


good outage management skills, with ability to multi-task


good understanding of all aspects of trading and order management flows in




            -   cash equity trading


-   operations & settlements


 


technical




5+ years fidessa application server experience


5+ years tcl scripting


5+ years unix and shell scripting skills


5+ years sql and sybase relational databases


good knowledge of fix protocol


understanding of software development lifecycle




 


key lateral relationships




equity traders


major business sponsors – heads of desks, trading, retail,


operational heads, compliance, audit, trade surveillance


development teams




 


units supported / key statistics




global equity
