purpose:


as rbc undertakes a major transformation to adopt agile and increase innovation bank wide, the agile community of practice is looking for an experienced agile coach to drive implementation of overall philosophy, strategy, tools and processes for wide spread adoption of the agile methodology and principles with various client teams. you will preferably be a csm and will be responsible for helping to ensure the success of agile projects across the rbc enterprise. you will be engaged in consulting, education and coaching support to, agile teams, scrum masters, product owners, the executive ranks and management throughout the rbc enterprise. the importance and necessity of this position will result in broad agile methodology adoption, corporate productivity improvements, enhanced project delivery and quality, cost optimization, speed to market and increased customer satisfaction.


all responsibilities carried out in accordance with code of conduct, rbc values and guiding principles ensuring adherence to privacy principles.


 


primary responsibilities
:


planning & strategy




trains agile teams and coaches them through the process of onboarding, initial project sprints and as needed throughout the project lifecycle


collects lessons learned through involvement with projects


participates in communications program including road shows, quality kickoff meetings and departmental presentations that will promote the principles and value of the agile community of practice


implements action items that will ensure the institutionalization of agile best practices across rbc


assists in determining which project methodologies are relevant to different complex projects  


researches emerging trends/best practices and determines implications of proposed methodologies on meeting business unit strategy


provides senior executives with management reports showing trends & key performance indicators for strategic agile initiatives across the enterprise




 


relationships & communication




effectively communicates and builds rapport with team members, stakeholders and business partners using a variety of techniques and collaboration from initiation to close


prepares and delivers presentations to business and technology partners, senior management and executives, selecting the appropriate approach based on the audience


resolves conflicts/issues and escalates others as appropriate


manages the operations with 3rd party vendors




 


managerial excellence




ensures team has adequate training, consulting and tools to deliver objectives


provides expertise, direction, coaching and development to build the team capability


promotes a mindset for sustained success and change championship


continually enhances own skills and builds knowledge in all aspects of the organization, the business and information systems




 


how time will be allocated (percent):




project planning - 20%


documentation and communication – 20%


execution/consulting services – 60%




 


authorities, impact, risk




changes in agile project management, governance processes and methodology which will have an impact on the majority of rbc delivery staff; so influence and impact is very significant






manages and prioritizes all responsibilities effectively


promotes quality, speed to market and cost-effectiveness




 


position requirements/ specifications


experience


minimum 5 years experience as a scrum master or agile coach


experience with application consulting firms would be preferred


demonstrated project management skills in delivering projects with 3rd party suppliers using agile methodology


thorough understanding of system development, managing vendor relationships and collaborating with internal, external partners


ability to apply agile methodology and best practices to a diverse range of projects


strong communication skills


strong analytical skills, problem solving skills


strong teamwork orientation


strong organizational skills with the ability to meet deadlines and manage several activities simultaneously


effective at working with senior managers and executives


proficient in ms office tool suite


 


education


undergraduate degree or equivalent experience


agile methodology training, certification preferred


 


technical & systems knowledge:


agile methodologies 


good organizational skills with the ability to handle several simultaneous activities of varying magnitude 


strong writing and communication skills 


strong stakeholder management skills


solid skills in managing issues, changes and problems 


ability to complete tasks independently 


issues escalation and resolution 


cost/benefit analysis & alternatives identification 


budgeting, project costs management 


 


domain knowledge


knowledge of agile methodology, standards, best practices, methodologies & audit requirements, as applicable


 


business knowledge


highly skilled at facilitation and information gathering 


general knowledge of factors affecting business


keeping up with trends in the agile practice
