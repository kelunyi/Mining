purpose:


 


engage it and business stakeholders to help plan, elicit, analyze, document, manage and communicate system requirements that meet business needs.


 


context: 
works on large development projects for the retail investments application (multiple functions, one application with multiple interfaces and/or 3
rd
 parties).


 


primary responsibilities:


 


business analysis, documentation & consulting




lead detailed system requirements phases and identify, track, and resolve business systems issues


assess, research, analyze, and document stakeholder needs, selecting the appropriate information gathering and/or modelling technique to elicit requirements


recommend business solutions that satisfy stakeholder needs, while continuously exploring and assessing options for value-add for clients


ensure successful completion of project objectives with high quality, within budget, and on time by identifying risks, dependencies, assumptions and constraints, and develop and recommend mitigation strategies


support development, qa and uat activities


review test plans, scenarios, cases as needed


identify, track, and resolve business systems issues


support post implementation verification (piv) as needed


together with project manager, determine best mix of development,  interfacing applications, and third party vendor solutions to meet business requirements and project objectives


provide consulting services to business partners on new initiatives


build and maintain a repository of reusable system documentation




 


planning & testing




develop detailed plans and accurate estimates for completion of system requirements and acceptance phases of projects, continuously improving the level of detail and accuracy of estimates




 


relationships & communication




effectively communicate, collaborate, and build rapport with team members, stakeholders, and business unit partners using a variety of techniques


plan and facilitate small- to large sized project meetings and conduct information gathering sessions


prepare and deliver presentations to business unit partners and senior management, selecting the appropriate approach based on the audience


resolve large complexity conflicts  and issues of medium complexity, appropriately escalate as needed


participate in evaluation of 3
rd
 – party vendors and software


manage some aspects of day-to-day operations with 3
rd
 – party vendors


identify and manage risks, dependencies, assumptions and constraints for requirements, and communicate to appropriate parties




 


 


development, mentoring & coaching




continually enhance skills and build knowledge in all aspects of the organization, the business, and information technology


provide expertise, training, direction, feedback, and coaching to build the capability of more junior staff

10+ years professional bsa experience in a project-based environment
