purpose


the   of this position is to support the rbc wealth management supplier management program and processes. the wm supplier management office leads the platform in driving flexible best practices for the management and oversight of supplier relationship globally, including defining roles, relationships, activities and tools. the incumbent will be responsible to lead and support the related platform activities and efforts on supplier research and analysis; monitoring and reporting; compliance related activity; and overall supplier relationship management program and processes


 


primary responsibilities


 


reporting




responsible for development, preparation, review and distribution of the supplier reporting needs of the wealth management platform, including: the third party risk, supplier management office status reporting, supplier compliance and due diligence reporting and standard spend reporting.


the support and preparation of ad hoc reporting as required




analysis




leadership and support of research and assessment activity regarding wealth management supplier base. for example: detailed regional supplier analysis.


preparation and/or compilation of analysis required in support of the above reporting requirements, including trending and status.


conducts ad hoc supplier research in support of new or expanding supplier relationships


manage a central library of suppliers and ensure the all required documentation and compliance activity is up to date and captured in the appropriate data bases and repositories




audit and compliance support




through appropriate status reporting, ensure supplier due diligence and compliance status is understood and in good order.


ensure compliance documentation can be sourced easily and assist in the compilation of required audit and regulatory documentation, as appropriate




supplier relationship management (srm) program support




act as a subject matter expert and provide direction to wealth management stakeholders on srm best practices and the supplier management tools (emptoris, service provider workbooks, etc)


fully understands the organizational resources, priorities, needs and policies including an understanding of osfi b10, the vog-1 policy and risk management approaches.




 


·         other projects, as assigned.


key competencies required


 




university degree or equivalent


experience in supplier relationship management highly preferred


high level understanding of rbc’s infrastructure services and organization


experience in mining and analysis of data and databases as well as the development/production of reports


effective collaborator and positive team member with strong interpersonal skills


strong customer service and consulting skills


strong presentation and coaching skills


strong planning, analytical & critical thinking, organizational, change management


acts with sense of urgency and demonstrates an ability to drive deliverables to competition


maturity level and skill/judgment to be able to deal effectively with senior managers


client-focused and process-driven, ability to build loyalty and trust by proactively anticipating needs / resolving issues

rbc enterprise services - supplier management group
