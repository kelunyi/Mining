purpose:


we are looking for a dynamic, enthusiastic individual to provide direction, guidance and support to all teams in the areas of automated testing and performance testing within our new devops coe. the succesful candidate must have a  consulting background or aptitude.


 


 


primary responsibilities:


 


planning & strategy




establish and evolve the enterprise performance and test automation standards processes and methodologies.


provide enterprise wide automated testing and performance testing consulting and training on complex application development or maintenance projects to team across the enterprise.


participate in planning and architectural review meetings for projects to ensuring all platform requirements pertaining to automated testing needs are met


evaluate testing tools to be used for performance and automated testing, execute proof of concepts


analyze projects & identify automation and performance test requirements based on technology & architecture


assist in developing and certifying performance strategies and plans based on technology & architecture


determine  types of automation and performance testing and  types of monitoring that may be required


create and evolve the performance and automated testing process from planning to closure and align it to the sdlc and pmf.


participate in evaluation of performance and automated test results


collaborate with other areas within it to identify and improve automated approaches based on technology and architecture of applications and systems


provide consulting at the enterprise level for automated and performance testing in the areas of tool selection, strategy, estimation, outsourcing and training


provides expertise, direction, coaching and development to build the team capability


continually enhances own skills and builds knowledge in all aspects of the organization, the business and information systems




 


relationships & communication




effectively communicates and builds rapport with team members, stakeholders and business partners using a variety of techniques and collaboration from initiation to close


facilitates complex group meetings and conducts information gathering sessions


prepares and delivers presentations to technology partners and senior management selecting the appropriate approach based on the audience


resolves conflicts/issues and escalates others as appropriate
